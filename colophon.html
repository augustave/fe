This volume gathers eight folios under one fixed form: doctrine on the recto, codex on the verso, a single mark spent once at the moment the two agree. One foundation that documents the form in its own body; a Norse trio that reads the rune poems as structural prediction rather than mysticism; a contemporary quartet that brackets a single bound action in its action, its authority, its origin, and its identity. The shape is closed by design — not stopped, but finished.

The Mark Color Index stands at seven of seven, every slot claimed and none reserved. This is a declaration, not an accident. The series argues for discipline, and a discipline that cannot end is no discipline at all. An eighth mark — and the folio that would carry it — is reachable only through a deliberate AUDIT: a re-architecture undertaken on purpose, never by routine build. Until such a decision is made and recorded, the index is shut.

Set in EB Garamond, JetBrains Mono, and Fraunces, all under the SIL Open Font License. The folios are zero-dependency HTML — no build step, no framework, no external asset beyond the type — bound into a single catalogue by a pipeline that renders each spread at its native dimensions and concatenates them in order. The form is older than this series; sovereignty has always operated through named, attested, legible artifacts. What the series demonstrates is that the form still works.
